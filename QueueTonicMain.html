Process

From booking to consultation in minutes.

A streamlined flow that respects everyone's time — patients, receptionists, and doctors alike.

Choose a doctor, describe symptoms, pick a slot. Urgency is scored instantly. Takes under 60 seconds.

2

Clinic Confirms

Reminders sent automatically. Live queue position shown in real-time. One-tap reschedule if delays occur.

4

Doctor Consults

Why QueueTonic
